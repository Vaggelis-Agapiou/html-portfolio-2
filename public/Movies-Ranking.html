Best Movies According to Vaggelis

My top 3 movies of all time

Akira

It is just mind boggling to me how they made such clear and articulate animations drawn just by hand in 1988, accompanied by a great action packed story.

The Lighthouse

A great and grim story based on the Character of Prometheus, who brought the fire to the humans against the will of the Gods.

Enter the Void

A jaw-dropping movie, that dwelves on what someone experiences at the moment of their own death.
